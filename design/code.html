Persian Grilled Saffron Chicken

local_fire_department 480 kcal
schedule Prep: 25 min
Protein 42g
Carbs 12g
Fat 28g

Ingredients

6 items

Instructions

1

Thinly slice onions and combine with yogurt, bloomed saffron, lemon juice, and salt in a large bowl.

2

Cut chicken into 2-inch cubes. Mix into marinade and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (ideally overnight).

3

Thread chicken onto flat skewers. Grill over high heat for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally until charred and juicy.
